Academic Work

Research

2025 · North South University
Accuracy–Robustness Tradeoff in Multi-Source Alzheimer's Disease Classification
  • Benchmarked 5 deep learning architectures (ResNet-18, MobileNet-V3, NeuroFusion, DeepCNN-SE-CBAM, SKTripletFusion) across 89,955 MRI images from 3 independent datasets
  • DeepCNN-SE-CBAM with dual-attention mechanisms achieved 91% real-world robustness under clinical noise conditions, outperforming 99%+ lab-accuracy models that collapsed under domain shift
  • Quantified accuracy-robustness tradeoffs across Kaggle, OASIS, and ADNI sources
2026 · North South University
MedT2V: Text-to-Medical Video Generation with Pyramidal Pseudo-Mamba Encoding & Direction-Aware Fusion
  • End-to-end text-to-video pipeline trained on 55,000 medical video-caption pairs using LoRA fine-tuning of Wan2.1-T2V-1.3B diffusion model
  • Engineered Pyramidal Pseudo-Mamba Encoding (PME) for multi-scale temporal modeling
  • Direction-Aware Fusion (DAF) module for enhanced text-video semantic alignment
